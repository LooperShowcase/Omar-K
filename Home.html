Cristino Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro. born 5 February 1985 is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both
Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five
Ballon d'Or awards
